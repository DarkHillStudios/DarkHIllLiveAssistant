About DarkHill Live Assistant

DarkHill Live Assistant is a cutting-edge tool designed to help live streamers enhance their streaming experience. With intuitive features for chat monitoring, audience engagement, and automation, the application aims to streamline the complex tasks of managing a live stream, allowing creators to focus on their content.
